Select a name and choose a class!

Warrior

Warriors are known for their strength and bravado in the field of battle. Boasting high strength and high health, they lay ruin to their enemys through strong but slow attacks and physical damage.

Rogue

Rogues are the darkness in the night. Shadows that materialize and disappear from thin air. While not as strong as their Warrior counterparts, they exploit the weakness their enemies themselves did not know.

Mage

Mages are wielders of great magicks and vicious curses. Mastery of intellect, they are susceptible to being physically weak, but only if the target can get close enough.
